El Ran • Home Theater Seating, Reclining Sectionals and Reclining Groups

Founded in 1967, EL RAN is a Canadian, family-owned company that specializes in motion furniture. A leader in its field, they offer a full range of reclining chairs, loveseats and sofas, as well as a wide selection of stationary upholstered furniture. EL RAN makes more than 150 models that run the gamut from current trends to more traditional and transitional styles. Collections are available in more than 550 fabrics and leathers.
