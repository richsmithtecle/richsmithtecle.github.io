Professional Outdoor Speaker Installation

Once you've heard your favorite songs on a high performance system, it's pretty hard to go back to that little bluetooth speaker on the patio. Mentor TV's Custom Installation Team can outfit your outdoor spaces with sophisticated sound solutions that last a lifetime. Designed to extend the listening environment from the patio to the garden, poolside and beyond, we can blend high-fidelity sound seamlessly into your surroundings.

Outdoor Speakers

Music is made for the outdoors, and good outdoor speakers make sure you're getting the most from it. With multiple brands, styles, and sizes, Mentor TV has the right option to perfect your "outdoor living room".

Planter Speakers

Planter Speakers are a great choice for outdoor music applications, again bringing indoor-quality sound to your outdoor oasis. These Planter Speakers (and yes, you can really use them as planters) come in terracotta and weathered concrete finishes.

Rock Speakers

Built like a tank, and plays like Mozart. Rock loudspeakers come in a variety of finishes to blend into any landscape.
