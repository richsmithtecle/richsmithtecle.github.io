Flat Panel TV &
Sound Bar Home Theater

Today's sound bar systems are better than ever and can deliver a great home theater experience. They are incredibly popular as they take up very little space and can be mounted to the wall or simply sit on a shelf below your TV. Select systems have the option to add "wireless" rear speakers for an even better surround sound experience. Sound bar systems are usually the ideal choice for small to medium sized rooms.

Flat Panel TV &
Separates Home Theater

A system built from separate components is the best way to experience home theater and your favorite music. Advancements in surround sound processing - such as Dolby Atmos - can deliver an immersive experience that rivals your local cineplex. These systems can utilize up to 11 small to large speakers, depending on the individual system. You'll also need a subwoofer, a home theater receiver, and a 4K Blu-ray. Component systems are usually the ideal choice for medium to large sized rooms.

Front Projector &
Separates Home Theater

When really big still isn't big enough - go with a front projector. They allow you to enjoy immersive cinematic experiences and sports with that up-close stadium effect as you expand your viewing possibilities with screen sizes from 85" up to 300". Front projection home theater systems are usually the ideal choice for medium to large sized rooms.
