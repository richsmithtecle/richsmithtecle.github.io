Professional flat-panel TV installation

So you bought a new flat-screen TV... Congratulations! Nothing enhances your entertainment experience like a huge flat-panel TV. You probably had to make a few choices along the way. What technology do I want? What size do I want or need? Here's another question we get asked quite often: "Should I wall mount my new TV?".

Here's three reasons why you should wall mount your TV:

Performance

Get the most out of your flat-panel TV by mounting it on the wall for optimal performance. Today's wall mounts can extend, tilt and swivel, making it easy to find the best viewing angle and reduce glare from lights or windows.

Space & Style

You can create valuable space on top of furniture or remove your furniture altogether for a clean, modern look in any room. Mounting your TV on the wall and out of the way is a great way to emphasize its sleek, thin design.

Safety

Protect your family by mounting your flat-panel TV securely on the wall. Its the smartest way to prevent accidental bumping or smudging.

Mentor TV offers professional delivery and wall mount installation of flat-panel TV's to customers in the Northeast Ohio area. This service typically includes our Installers quickly and professionally unpacking and setting up the TV, installing the wall mount bracket and securely attaching the TV, concealing wires inside the wall and neatly dressing any exposed wires.
