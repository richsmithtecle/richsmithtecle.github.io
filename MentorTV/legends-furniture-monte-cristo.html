Monte Cristo Entertainment Center

The Legends Monte Cristo Entertainment Center is a gorgeous three piece wall unit consisting of a 55-inch wide TV Stand base, two lighted curio cabinets, and a sturdy bridge with back panel. This beautiful wall creates a classic presence in your home, and features traditional design features including crown moulding top and carved detailed edges. Constructed from top quality hardwood, this beautiful entertainment center features a rich distressed Chestnut finish. A combination of open and closed storage provides ample room for all of your AV components and related accessories. A majestic way to enhance the look and function of your home entertainment room.

Entertainment Wall Includes:
55" TV Console (55.5"W x 17.5"D x 30"H)
Lighted Curio Pier Cabinets (x2) (15.75"W x 13.75"D x 65"H)
