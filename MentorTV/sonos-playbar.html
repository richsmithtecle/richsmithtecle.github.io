Sonos PLAYBAR • Stunningly simple.

No Headache Setup

PLAYBAR is quick and easy to set up. Just plug the power cord in, attach the optical cord to your TV and connect to your home wireless network router.

Easy to control

Control TV volume using your existing IR remote(s). Download free Sonos apps to control PLAYBAR with your iPhone, iPad or Android device and stream your music, even when your TV is off.

Sonos grows with you

PLAYBAR is perfect on its own but ready to welcome any new Sonos additions into the fold. Add a Sonos SUB for soul-shaking sound, or add in a pair of wireless PLAY:1's for a more immersive surround sound experience.
