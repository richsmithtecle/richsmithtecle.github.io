Exceptional Performance.

Since 1910, Denon has continued to introduce the most innovative audio / video products with the latest technologies, superb craftsmanship and the leading performance worldwide. The company is one of the world's most diversified specialist audio and home theater providers who offer consumers and professionals breathtaking sound and images, superior reliability and unrivaled convenience features.

Denon CI Certified. The highest level of expertise and service.

With the advent of their "IN-Command Series", Denon created a dedicated program for their custom integration dealers. Only Custom Integrators that complete their annual and extensive CI Certification program can use this logo, whether it be on their websites, marketing materials etc. This Certification program includes many hours of in person training, as well as follow up on-line sessions. It is Denon's 'Seal of Expertise' for you, that Mentor TV has proven our ability to sell, integrate and service our 'CI' products. We also have access to many special added services to make your purchase, installation and use of Denon products as easy and enjoyable as possible.

Denon X-Series. Fully loaded home theater receivers.

In addition to the latest surround sound technology and the ultimate in connectivity, Denon's X Series models let music lovers listen to their favorite tracks wirelessly from portable music players, smartphones and tablet devices. These models make accessing audio via home Wi-Fi networks easier and better than ever with a broad selection of Internet radio stations, and stream tracks from subscription services such as Pandora, SiriusXM and Spotify Connect.
