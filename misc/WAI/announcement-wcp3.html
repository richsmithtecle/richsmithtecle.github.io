New WAI Client Access Portal

We are excited to announce the arrival of the WAI Client Access Portal. The client portal puts your organization more in control of the assessment process with 24-hour access to codes and reports.

Create Assessment Codes

Whether you need one assessment or twenty-five, create the assessment codes directly in the portal to be used immediately, later, or both. Select the report you require, and the corresponding assessment is automatically populated.

Send codes to Candidates

No separate email required! Send the assessment information directly to the candidate from the portal. All you need is the candidate name and email address. You can send the code directly after you create it or later.

View Codes Status

The portal helps you to track the status of your assessment codes with three categories: Available, Sent, and In Progress. Each report has a separate section for easy viewing.

Run Reports

Missing a report on someone? Want to see how a previous test-taker looks in a different job? You can run reports directly from the client portal. *Some reports may have an additional fee.

User Control

Several user levels available from Administrator to Limited access. Allows multiple people within your organization to access the portal with full control or limited access for certain functions.
